# Key Features oh LALA company

## Joining Process

- They directly call you for an interview and send the location via WhatsApp.
- A random person conducts the interview.
- Candidates are judged based on experience.
- Previous income is asked to decide the salary.
- Immediate joining is required.
- No joining letter is provided for 15–30 days.
- A 2-year bond is mandatory.
- Original documents and a signed blank cheque are required.

---

## Work Culture

- Entry time is **8:00 AM**. Even a **1-minute delay** is considered a **half-day leave**.
- Leaving before **6:00 PM** is considered a **full-day leave**.
- Office travel expenses must be paid by the employee.
- If leave is taken on **Saturday or Monday**, it is counted as **two days**.
- Lunch break is limited to **30 minutes**.
- **6–7 working days** per week.
- Office size is approximately **2.6 × 6**, with only **one AC**.
- Tea is provided by an outside vendor; employees must buy their own biscuits.
- Employees must remain silent; talking is not allowed.
- No employee stays longer than **2–3 months**.
- Salary is credited after **15 days**.
- Any colleague can open or close the office.
- No fixed or assigned job roles.
- Freshers are hired for **3 months of training** without any trainer.
- Expectations and required experience are high, but salary is low.
- Employees are expected to handle **multiple roles**, including:
  - Sales  
  - Client meetings  
  - Development  
  - Testing  
  - Desktop support  
  - Networking  
  - SEO
- Salary is deducted if the company faces losses.

---

## During Resignation

- Employees can be terminated at any time without notice.
- Employees cannot resign immediately; **15 days’ prior notice** is mandatory.
- **10–15 days’ salary** is deducted during resignation.
